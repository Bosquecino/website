Project Cristobal

The project Cristobal was leading by Alex Pajero between 2017 and 2018. It was a concept car presented in the Smart City Congress 2017 and the Mobile World Congress 2018 with the motto "Vision Zero" and the goal of accident avoidance.

The vehicle had 17 innovative functions, some of them are:
· Eye Tracker to detect tiredness and distractions.
· Breathalyzer connected to the start engine system to avoid drunk drivers.
· Mentor App, to track the vehicle's position and speed compared with the maximal road speed.
· Settings like maximal speed or allowed zone can be controlled from the Smartphone throught the Mentor App.
· Speed Limitation according to the maximal road speed.
· Black Box with camera images and vehicle's data
· Child Reminder Function to contact the user in case of living beings have been forgotten in car.

Alex Parejo presenting the concept car Cristobal in the Mobile World Congress 2018 for the national TV channel from Spain.

Education

First Vocational Education (2002 – 2004, Seville)

Since he was a child, Alex Parejo has always been interested in electronics. He disassembled, repaired and modified devices that came to his hands. Due to this interest in technology, in September 2002 he started vocational education in field Control and Regulation of Automatic Systems.

During this period he acquired knowledge about electronics, electrical installations and programming of programmable logic controllers (PLC).

Second Vocational Education (2005 – 2007, Seville)

After one year as technical reparing laboratory equipment, in september 2005 Parejo decided to realize a second vocational education in field Technical Drawing in order to bring his career to the designing of electronical devices.

During this period he acquired knowledge about technical drawing, architecture, civil engineering and topography.

Bachelor of Engineering (2009 – 2013, Berlin)

In 2008 Parejo moved to Germany. After some jobs as technical drawer, Parejo take a leap in his professional career starting a Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Communication Systems at the Beuth University of Applied Sciences Berlin in April 2009. In spite of the difficulty of realizing a studies in a foreign language and working part time as technical drawer, Parejo finished the engineering degree with excellent grades, 1.85 by the german grading system and about 3.8 by the GPA U.S. system.

Parejo chose these studies because of they covered the main technology fields like electronics, telecommunications and programming.

His thesis was Selection Process and Development of an Application for SEAT S.A. (Volkswagen Group), getting the maximal possible grade for it, 1.0 in the german grading system.

Master of Science (2013 – currently, Hagen)

After finishing the Bachelor, Alex Parejo continued his education starting a Master of Science in Computer Science with focus in Information Systems and Artificial Intelligence at the University of Hagen in october 2013. This is a distance degree since Parejo worked at the same time full time in the Innovation Department of SEAT. In order to be able to realice distance studies while working some skills like self-discipline, self-taught, organized and thirst for knowledge are necessary. Because of Parejo is focused mainly on his job and studies in his free time, his availibity is reduced dependig of his workload. The graduation is expected for September 2019. His current middle grade is 1.2 by the german grading system and about 4.0 by the GPA U.S. system.
